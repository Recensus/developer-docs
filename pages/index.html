Build With Recensus

Welcome to the Recensus developer documentation. This site includes all the information required to integrate the Recensus review widget into your ecommerce website and use the Recensus APIs to communicate with your customers.

In addition to the various APIs and SDKs offered by Recensus you will also find a number of integration packs for popular ecommerce frameworks.
